About Me

Samantha Tyler Samantha qualified from Middlesbrough College in 2010 and set up as a self-employed Beauty Therapist, offering a convenient mobile service within the community. With a desire to develop her skills further and expand her business, Samantha went on to study body and spa therapy and successfully achieved her Diploma in 2011. In 2012 Samantha moved South to Bedfordshire and took some time out to settle her young family into a new area and new schools. During this period, she offered to do free charity work for an organization that supports families with children and young people who are on the autistic spectrum. This work allowed Samantha to offer something to her new community whilst allowing the time to support her young children. In 2017 Samantha started her journey into massage and holistic therapy. Over the last 2 years She has established a successful business dedicated to massage and holistic Treatments. Samantha prides herself on offering a supportive service to her clients, helping them feel positive, relaxed calm and energized through the massage and holistic experience. Away from work Samantha likes to keep fit and enjoys spending time with her family, she is a regular side-line parent at both football and rugby on a weekend. Qualifications NVQ Level 2 Beauty Therapy - 2010 NVQ Level 3 Diploma in Body and Spa Therapy - 2011 Indian Head Massage Diploma - 2017 Currently Studying BTCT Level 3 Diploma in reflexology
